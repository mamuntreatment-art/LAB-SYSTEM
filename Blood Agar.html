Blood Agar Growth

Blood Agar একটি enriched media যা বিভিন্ন bacteria এর growth ও hemolysis pattern দেখার জন্য ব্যবহৃত হয়। বিশেষ করে urinary pathogens এবং pathogenic bacteria identify করতে এটি গুরুত্বপূর্ণ।

Types of Hemolysis

Hemolysis Type Description Appearance
Beta Hemolysis Complete RBC destruction Clear transparent zone around colony
Alpha Hemolysis Partial RBC destruction Greenish discoloration around colony
Gamma Hemolysis No hemolysis No change around colony

Common Organisms on Blood Agar

Organism Colony Appearance Hemolysis
Staphylococcus aureus Golden yellow colony Beta Hemolysis
Streptococcus pyogenes Small translucent colony Beta Hemolysis
Streptococcus pneumoniae Mucoid colony Alpha Hemolysis
Enterococcus spp. Gray white colony Gamma Hemolysis
Escherichia coli Gray moist colony Sometimes Beta
Pseudomonas aeruginosa Green pigment colony Beta Hemolysis

Biochemical Tests for Identification

Test Purpose Positive Example
Gram Stain Gram positive / negative identification All bacteria
Catalase Test Staphylococcus vs Streptococcus differentiation Staphylococcus aureus
Coagulase Test S. aureus confirmation Staphylococcus aureus
Bacitracin Sensitivity Streptococcus pyogenes identification S. pyogenes
Optochin Sensitivity Streptococcus pneumoniae identification S. pneumoniae
Bile Esculin Test Enterococcus detection Enterococcus spp.
Oxidase Test Pseudomonas detection Pseudomonas aeruginosa
Important: Blood agar enriched media হওয়ায় fastidious organisms grow করতে পারে। Hemolysis pattern organism identification এর একটি গুরুত্বপূর্ণ feature।
