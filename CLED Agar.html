CLED Agar

CLED Agar এর পূর্ণরূপ হলো Cystine Lactose Electrolyte Deficient Agar। এটি urine culture এর জন্য ব্যবহৃত একটি special culture media।

Purpose of CLED Agar

  • Urinary pathogens isolate করা
  • Lactose fermenter ও non-lactose fermenter differentiate করা
  • Proteus species এর swarming growth prevent করা

Common Organisms on CLED Agar

Organism Colony Appearance Lactose Fermentation
Escherichia coli Yellow colony Positive
Klebsiella spp. Mucoid yellow colony Positive
Enterococcus spp. Small yellow colony Positive
Proteus spp. Blue / pale colony Negative
Pseudomonas aeruginosa Greenish blue colony Negative
Salmonella spp. Pale blue colony Negative

Biochemical Tests for Identification

Test Purpose Positive Example
Gram Stain Gram positive / negative differentiation All bacteria
Indole Test E. coli identification E. coli
Oxidase Test Pseudomonas detection Pseudomonas aeruginosa
Urease Test Proteus identification Proteus spp.
Citrate Test Klebsiella differentiation Klebsiella spp.
TSI Test Sugar fermentation pattern Enterobacteriaceae
Important: CLED agar electrolyte deficient হওয়ায় Proteus species এর swarming growth কমে যায়, যার ফলে অন্যান্য bacteria সহজে identify করা যায়।
