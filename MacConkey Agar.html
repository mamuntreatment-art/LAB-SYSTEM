MacConkey Agar Growth

MacConkey Agar একটি selective এবং differential media। এটি Gram-negative enteric bacteria isolate ও lactose fermentation detect করার জন্য ব্যবহৃত হয়।

Purpose of MacConkey Agar

  • Gram-negative bacteria grow করানো
  • Gram-positive bacteria inhibit করা
  • Lactose fermenter ও non-lactose fermenter differentiate করা

Common Organisms on MacConkey Agar

Organism Colony Appearance Lactose Fermentation
Escherichia coli Bright pink colony Positive
Klebsiella spp. Large mucoid pink colony Positive
Enterobacter spp. Pink moist colony Positive
Citrobacter spp. Pink colony Positive
Proteus spp. Colorless colony Negative
Pseudomonas aeruginosa Pale / colorless colony Negative
Salmonella spp. Colorless colony Negative
Shigella spp. Transparent colorless colony Negative

Lactose Fermentation Interpretation

Fermentation Type Colony Color Example
Lactose Fermenter Pink / Red colony E. coli, Klebsiella
Non-Lactose Fermenter Colorless / Pale colony Proteus, Salmonella

Biochemical Tests for Identification

Test Purpose Positive Example
Gram Stain Gram-negative bacilli identification Enterobacteriaceae
Indole Test E. coli identification E. coli
Urease Test Proteus detection Proteus spp.
Citrate Test Klebsiella differentiation Klebsiella spp.
Oxidase Test Pseudomonas detection Pseudomonas aeruginosa
TSI Test Sugar fermentation & H₂S production Salmonella spp.
Motility Test Bacterial motility detection Proteus spp.
Important: MacConkey agar এ bile salts এবং crystal violet থাকার কারণে Gram-positive bacteria এর growth inhibit হয়। Neutral red indicator lactose fermentation detect করতে সাহায্য করে।
