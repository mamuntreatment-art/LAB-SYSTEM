Urine Culture Organism Identification

Urine culture এ সাধারণত CLED agar, Blood agar এবং MacConkey agar ব্যবহার করা হয়। এই media গুলোতে বিভিন্ন organism এর growth pattern দেখে organism identify করা হয়।

CLED Agar Growth

Organism Colony Appearance Lactose Fermentation
Escherichia coli Yellow colony Positive
Klebsiella spp. Large mucoid yellow colony Positive
Proteus spp. Pale blue colony Negative
Pseudomonas aeruginosa Greenish blue colony Negative
Enterococcus spp. Small yellow colony Positive

Blood Agar Growth

Organism Hemolysis Pattern Appearance
Staphylococcus aureus Beta Hemolysis Golden yellow colony
Streptococcus pyogenes Beta Hemolysis Small translucent colony
Enterococcus spp. Gamma Hemolysis Gray white colony
Escherichia coli Sometimes Beta Hemolysis Gray moist colony
Pseudomonas aeruginosa Beta Hemolysis Green pigment colony

MacConkey Agar Growth

Organism Colony Color Lactose Fermentation
Escherichia coli Bright pink colony Positive
Klebsiella spp. Mucoid pink colony Positive
Enterobacter spp. Pink moist colony Positive
Proteus spp. Colorless colony Negative
Pseudomonas aeruginosa Pale/colorless colony Negative
Salmonella spp. Colorless colony Negative

Biochemical Tests for Identification

Test Purpose Positive Organism
Gram Stain Gram positive / negative identification All bacteria
Catalase Test Staphylococcus detection Staphylococcus aureus
Coagulase Test S. aureus confirmation Staphylococcus aureus
Indole Test E. coli identification E. coli
Oxidase Test Pseudomonas detection Pseudomonas aeruginosa
Urease Test Proteus detection Proteus spp.
Citrate Test Klebsiella differentiation Klebsiella spp.
TSI Test Sugar fermentation pattern Enterobacteriaceae
Motility Test Bacterial movement detect Proteus spp.
Germ Tube Test Candida albicans শনাক্ত Candida albicans
Note: Final organism identification করার জন্য colony morphology, Gram stain, biochemical tests এবং antibiotic sensitivity pattern একসাথে evaluate করা হয়।
Note: Mueller-Hinton agar মূলত sensitivity testing এর জন্য standard media। Primary isolation এর জন্য সাধারণত CLED agar, Blood agar এবং MacConkey agar ব্যবহার করা হয়।
