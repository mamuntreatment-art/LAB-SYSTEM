SEMEN ANALYSIS FORMOLA

🧮 ১. Morphology (%)

👉 এটা ল্যাবের মূল হিসাব

   Morphology (%) =  (  Normal Forms Total Counted  )  × 100    

📌 উদাহরণ
Normal Forms = 10, Total counted = 200
➡️ (10 ÷ 200) × 100 = 5%

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

🧮 ২. Direct Formula (এক ধাপে)

   Normal Sperm =  (  Normal Forms Total Counted  )  × Total Concentration    

Normal Sperm=( Normal Forms / Total Counted )×Total Concentration


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

৩. Motility Breakdown Formula

   Total Motility = Actively Motile + Feeble Motile       Total = Actively + Feeble + Non-Motile    

📌 উদাহরণ
Actively = 40%
Feeble = 20%
Non-motile = 40%

➡️ Total Motility = 60%
